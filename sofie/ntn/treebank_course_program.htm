Details and Ideas

  1. MV: Welcome; Overview, Motivation
    • The Nordic Treebank Network
    • My Treebank Experience for PP attachment disambiguation
    • The history of treebanks (Penn Treebank, SynTag, Negra, ...)
    • Treebanks at the heart of the Machine Learning paradigm
  2. SGC: Corpus Collection and Preparation
    • How to start a corpus? How to start a treebank?
    • Corpus collection and representation
    • Different text genres
    • Written vs. spoken language
  3. Computerlab: Annotating a Treebank
    • Core task: Annotate 30 English sentences with Annotate according to the Penn Treebank Guidelines
    • Side tasks: Annotate sentences of your own language (Estonian, Finnish, Danish, Norwegian, Swedish) with Annotate in the spirit of the Penn Treebank Guidelines
  4. SGC: Querying a Treebank
    • What can a linguist get out of a treebank?
    • Introduction to the TIGERSearch query language
    • Introduction and comparison to Tgrep
  5. MV: Treebank Tools
    • Treebank Preparation tools (PoS-tagger, parser, chunker)
    • Treebank Editors and Disambiguator (Annotate, Redwood Tools)
    • Treebank Search Tools
  6. Computerlab: Treebank Queries and Treebank Extraction
    • programmer task: extract PP 5-tuples from the Penn treebank with Perl
    • intermediate task: extract PP 5 tuples from the Penn treebank with Tgrep
    • non-programmer task: complex queries in TIGER-Search (comments and examples in HTML page)
  7. MV: Treebank Usage
    • Training a chunker on a treebank
    • Evaluation a parser against a treebank
    • Treebanks for language learning (linguistics education)
  8. SGC: Treebank Maintenance
    • Error detection
    • Consistency checking
    • Treebank enrichment
    • Treebank conversion
    • Version control
  9. Computerlab: Training a Parser
    • programmer task: train a parser on the Penn treebank
    • non-programmer task: describe an algorithm for enriching the Penn treebank with anaphoric references, semantic tags, ...
  10. SGC: Treebanks and Discourse Information
    • What kind of discourse analysis?
    • Discourse analysis for spoken and written language
  11. E. Hinrichs: Spoken Language Treebanks
    • The specific aspects of spoken language treebanks vs. written language treebanks
    • The Verbmobil treebank
    • Other spoken language treebanks (Switchboard, ...)
  12. Computerlab: Treebank Conversion
    • programmer task: write a conversion program along the lines described by J. Nivre for converting a constituent treebank to a dependency treebank
    • non-programmer task: study the conversion of NEGRA to LFG and describe it in detail
  13. M. Trautner: The Danish Dependeny Treebank
    • How to compile an annotation manual?
    • Practical aspects of treebank building: How many sentences per hour?
    • The specific aspects of the Danish Dependeny Treebank
  14. MV: The future of Treebanks
    • Co-reference annotation
    • Semantic annotation
    • Parallel treebanks
    • Treebanks, data-oriented parsing and translation memory systems

 

Martin Volk; 28. November 2003
