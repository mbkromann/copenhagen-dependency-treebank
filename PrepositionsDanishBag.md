## The Danish preposition "bag" ##

**BAG** Occurs only with nominal complements

  * Special constructions
    * dukke frem bag avisen
    * gå over bag teltet
  * Adverbial uses (tagged as RG)
    * bag i bilen, bag ved huset
    * komme bag på
    * Ligge bag, stå bag
