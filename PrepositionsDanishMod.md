## The Danish preposition "mod" ##

**MOD** Occurs with full range of complements.

  * Special constructions
    * gå {frem/hen/op/ind/over/ud} **mod** byen
    * op **mod** 100 mennesker
  * Fixed expressions
    * på vej mod
  * Adverbial uses (tagged as RG): None!
