## Prepositions Danish Under ##

**UNDER** Occurs only with nominal complements!

  * Special constructions
    * på **under** 10 minutter
  * Adverbial uses (tagged as RG)
    * skrive under (på X)
    * bukke under (for X)
    * gå under
